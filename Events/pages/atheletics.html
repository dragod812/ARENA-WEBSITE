Athletics

Men

  • Track Events: 100m, 200m, 400m, 3k , 5k , 4*100m Relay
  • Field Events : Shotput

Women

  • Track Events: 100m, 200m, 2k, 4*100m Relay

Mixed

  • Track Events: 4*100m Relay

Rules:

  • Heats will be conducted only for 100m and 200m races, based on which participants will be selected to compete in the final race.
  • All other races will have no heats. The participants will be timed and the results will be decided accordingly.
  • Maximum 3 participants from each college in any particular event are allowed (excluding relays , which requires 4 per college team).
  • An individual can participate in maximum 3 individual events (excluding shotput).
  • An event may stand cancelled in case of insufficient participation.
  • Decision of the Referee/Event Manager is final in case of any discrepancy
