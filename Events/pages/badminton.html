Badminton

Boys:

  • Each team comprises of minimum 4 and maximum 5 players.
  • It will be best 3 of 5 matches. Each match comprises of best 2 of 3 sets. Each set will be of 21 points along with deuce (if any).
  • There will be 2 doubles and 3 singles events between opposing teams out of which , the team who wins any three events first wins the game.
  • Yonex Aerosensa-2 Feather shuttlecocks will be used to conduct badminton matches in the tournament.

Girls:

  • Each team comprises of minimum 2 and maximum 3 players.
  • It will be best 2 of 3 matches. Each match comprises of best 2 of 3 sets. Each set will be of 21 points along with deuce (if any).
  • There will be 1 doubles and 2 singles events between opposing teams out of which ,the team who wins any two events first wins the game.
  • Yonex Aerosensa-2 Feather shuttlecocks will be used to conduct badminton matches in the tournament.
