Cricket

Rules

  • Tournament comprises of Group stage, Quarter - finals, Semi - finals and Finals.
  • Maximum of 15 players are allowed per team.
  • Tournament will be played in T-20 format and normal T-20 rules apply.
  • Tournament comprises of a Group stage,Semi-Finals and Finals.
  • Tie in the game will be resolved by a super over.
  • Tie in case of points or wins will be resolved by comparison of ' net runrates of all the matches ' played by the team.
  • Teams should report 15 minutes before the scheduled time if the team is late by more than 10 minutes, the match would be forfeited to the opposition team.
  • Team has to complete its quota within 1hour 25 minutes.
  • If the 1st bowling team fails to complete then they will be penalized and have to chase the score in the number of overs they have bowled in the specified time.
  • If the 2nd bowling team fails to complete then runs equal to (current run rate of the batting team * the no of overs) left will be added to the batting side total,also they will have to bowl the overs left.
  • Decision of Umpires and Organizing committee shall be final and binding.
