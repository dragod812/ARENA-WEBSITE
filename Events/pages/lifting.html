Power Lifting

Weight classes

  • Above 67.5 to 75kgs.
  • Above 75 to 82.5kgs.
  • Above 82.5 to 90kgs.
  • Above 90 to 100kgs
  • for all the lifts you will be given 3 chances and the best of the lifts will be considered.And the sum of the weights is considered

rules for dead lift:

  • After the referee gives a call,you will have 1 min to start the lift.(weights being already loaded).
  • you can make mutliple attempts with in that minute as long as you start lifting the bar in the given time.
  • after the lift,referee gives a down call,after ensuring that your shoulders are sufficiently back ie normal position and your knees were locked
    • .
  • while placing the weight on thw floor your hands must be in contact with the rod,though you need not have to do a slow eccentric and you don’t have to lower the bar down completely under control.
  • you should not move your feet at any point during the lift.
  • finally,no hitching and ramping ie you should not support the weight with your thighs on the way to lock out. If your knees come forward just a little after the bar passes them, you’re okay. If your knees shoot way forward, and rebend a great deal,you will be called for ramping. hitching is strictly prohibited ie ramping multiple times.

rules for bench press:

  • before you start thelift following must be ensured.
    head is on the bench,
    shoulders are on the bench,
    butt is on the bench and
    feet are on the ground.
  • you should begin the lift with in a min of your call.you can take the help of a spotter to help on the lift of.
  • you must lower the bar until it touch any part of your body and u should hold it there motionless until you hear the press command.
  • Then the press the bar up until your elbows are locked and then you can rack the rod.
  • during the lift you must not leave the contact of the above mentioned parts.
  • your maximum grip can be upto 81 cms apart ie Your forefingers must be in contact with the rings.

rules for the squat:

  • u must start unracking the loaded bar with in 1 min of your call.if u are using stands you must walk it out.
  • then you must go as deep as your hip goes lower than the knee joint then u will be given the squat command and you were good to go.
  • after the squat you will be given rack command
  • you should not move your feet or touch your knee joints at any point during the squat.
