Squash

Rules:

  • Each team to have (3 +1) players i.e. minimum 3 and maximum 4. A single college can submit any number of teams.
  • Members of the team and the order in which they will play is to be decided before start of play and cannot be changed thereafter. If a player retires due to any circumstance the order of play slides upward. Arbitrary placement of players on retirement is not allowed.
  • A match will be a best of 5 sets.
  • Each set is of 11 points played at point per rally system (PAR).
  • Standard WSF rules will be followed and decision of the referee will be final and binding.
  • There will be an open event too where the registration can be done on spot.
