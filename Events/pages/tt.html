Table Tennis

The Table Tennis competition will have 2 events: Boys’ team and Girls' team.

Boy's Team

  • Minimum of 4 and a maximum of 5 players are allowed per team.
  • Each team match will be best of five individual matches.
  • Order of play of each of these individual matches is as follows:
    • 2 Singles; 1 Doubles; 2 reverse-singles in the standard ABC vs. XYZ format.
    • i.e., A-X, B-Y, C-Z, A-Y and B-X.
  • Each individual match will be played best of 5 sets of 11 points each.
  • The players playing singles won’t be allowed to play doubles.
  • All matches would be played with Stag 3 star Peter Karlsson- white plastic balls.
  • For any in-game arguments, the referee’s call would be final.

Girls'Team

  • 3 players per team.
  • Team matches will be held in the standard ABC vs. XYZ format. There won’t be any doubles matches.
  • Order of play of each of these individual matches is as follows:
    • During League Matches:

      Each team match will be a best of 3 individual matches as follows:
      A vs. X; B vs. Y; C vs. Z.

    • During Knockout Matches:

      Each team match will be a best of 5 individual matches as follows:
      A vs. X; B vs. Y; C vs. Z; A vs. Y; B vs. X.

  • Each individual match will be played best of 5 sets of 11 points each.
  • All matches would be played with Stag 3 star Peter Karlsson- white plastic balls.
  • For any in-game arguments, the referee’s call would be final.

Ranking Format for both Events (in case of a tie in the group stages):

  • In case 2 teams have the same number of victories at the end of the group stage, the team with the least sets lost will be ranked higher.
  • If the above criterion is still not sufficient, the team with the least points lost will be ranked higher
